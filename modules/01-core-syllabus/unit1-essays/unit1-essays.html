Unit 1: Academic Essays

Academic essays are not just longer paragraphs — they are structured arguments with a clear communicative purpose. In this unit you will explore the different types of academic essays, learn how to build a solid essay structure, and practice the core skills of summarizing and paraphrasing from sources.
